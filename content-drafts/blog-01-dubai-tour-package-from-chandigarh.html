Dubai Visa for Indian Citizens: Process & Documents

The UAE tourist visa is one of the easiest international visas for Indians — and it is fully handled by our visa desk in Mohali when you book a package. Standard processing takes 3–4 working days; we recommend applying at least 2 weeks before departure to stay safe. Documents you need:

  • Passport scan (front + back) with at least 6 months validity from the travel date
  • One passport-size photograph with a white background
  • Confirmed return flight tickets (we generate these with your booking)
  • Hotel confirmation (included in the package)
  • PAN card copy (required by most UAE visa processors for Indian applicants)

No embassy visit, no interview — everything is filed online. Tourist visas are available for 30 days (standard) and 60 days (extended stays), and we also arrange 96-hour transit visas for short stopovers.

9 Money-Saving Tips from Our Booking Desk

Two decades of booking Dubai from Chandigarh teaches you where the savings hide. Steal these:

  1. Book 45–60 days out. This is the sweet spot where direct-flight fares and hotel rates are both at their lowest.
  2. Fly the direct IXC flight. A Delhi departure may look slightly cheaper until you add the taxi, tolls, meals, and the airport-hotel night — the direct flight usually wins.
  3. Travel in shoulder season. April and late September deliver 25–35% lower hotel rates with perfectly usable weather.
  4. Take combo attraction tickets. Burj Khalifa + Dubai Aquarium or Atlantis combos cost meaningfully less than separate tickets.
  5. Use a forex card, not airport exchange. Load AED in India before you fly; airport counters in both countries offer the worst rates.
  6. Eat where Dubai eats. Al Karama and Deira serve excellent Indian and Arabic food at one-third of Downtown restaurant prices.
  7. Ride the Metro. A Nol card makes the Red Line a fraction of taxi cost — it stops at Dubai Mall, Marina, and the Expo district.
  8. Book the desert safari in your package, not at the hotel desk. Hotel concierge desks resell the same safari at a 40–60% markup.
  9. Hunt free attractions. The Dubai Fountain show, JBR beach walk, Gold Souk, and Friday markets cost nothing and fill half a day.
